Pokemon Collector!


You will be given number at the start of the game.

There are four Pokemons below. By clicking on a Pokemon you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each Pokemon are hidden from you until you click on them.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the value of each Pokemon.
